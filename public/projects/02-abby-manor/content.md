# Abby Manor Project

**Published:** January 2, 2024  
**URL:** https://parkwayeastdesign.com/abby-manor-project/  
**Type:** Residential - Custom Home  
**Location:** Stone Cliff, St. George, Utah

---

## Project Overview

Filled with sentimental pieces, pops of bright color, and international influences, this gorgeous custom home built in Stone Cliff, St. George, is a variation of a modern farmhouse. The design balances sophisticated farmhouse aesthetics with personal touches that make it uniquely the client's own.

## Featured Spaces

### Kitchen & Dining Room
The heart of the home featuring custom cabinetry, thoughtful storage solutions, and a dining area perfect for family gatherings and entertaining.

### Living Room & Entryway
Welcoming spaces that set the tone for the entire home, incorporating those signature pops of bright color and international influences throughout.

### Master Suite
A private retreat designed for comfort and relaxation, featuring carefully curated furnishings and a spa-like bathroom.

### Bunkroom
A fun and functional space designed for hosting guests and creating memories, with multiple sleeping arrangements and playful design elements.

---

## Design Highlights

- **Style:** Modern Farmhouse variation / Sophisticated Farmhouse
- **Color Palette:** Bright pops of color against neutral bases
- **Influences:** International design elements
- **Special Features:** Sentimental pieces incorporated throughout
