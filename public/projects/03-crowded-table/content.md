## Project Overview

At Crowded Table, deliberate design and refined finishes unfold across 9,019 square feet of seamless indoor-outdoor living. Five private bedroom suites, two thoughtfully designed levels, and generous gathering spaces create a home centered on connection. Framed by Southern Utah's expansive vistas, a resort-style pool and outdoor living areas invite moments of pause and togetherness from day to night.

**Featured in the 2025 St. George Parade of Homes.**

## Featured Spaces

### Great Room
The central gathering space designed for connection and entertaining, featuring dramatic ceiling heights and seamless flow to outdoor areas.

### Kitchen
A chef's dream with high-end finishes, generous counter space, and thoughtful storage solutions for serious entertaining.

### Dining Room
Elegant dining space designed to accommodate large gatherings, embodying the "crowded table" concept at the heart of this home.

Functional yet beautiful utility space with premium finishes.

### Bedroom Suite

### Downstairs Suite
A complete guest suite on the lower level for extended family or visitors.

### Guest Bedroom
Comfortable accommodations for overnight guests.

### Double Twin Suite

## Design Philosophy

The name "Crowded Table" reflects the home's core purpose: creating spaces for connection and togetherness. Every design decision, from the generous gathering areas to the seamless indoor-outdoor transitions, was made with hospitality and family in mind.
