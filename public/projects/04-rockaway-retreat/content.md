## Project Overview

This home brings Rockaway Beach inspiration inside with warm woods, textured fabrics, and calming greens. Every room balances comfort and refinement, creating spaces that feel open, grounded, and welcoming. Designed for hosting and connection, it embraces natural light and easy indoor–outdoor living.

## Design Philosophy

Inspired by the relaxed coastal vibes of Rockaway Beach, this home creates a retreat atmosphere while maintaining the sophistication expected in a high-end custom home. The design emphasizes:

- **Warm Woods** - Natural wood elements throughout create warmth and organic beauty
- **Textured Fabrics** - Layered textures add depth and visual interest
- **Calming Greens** - A soothing color palette centered on nature-inspired greens
- **Natural Light** - Maximized windows and open layouts flood spaces with light
- **Indoor-Outdoor Living** - Seamless transitions between interior and exterior spaces

## Featured Spaces

### Great Room
The heart of the home featuring dramatic ceiling heights, warm wood accents, and comfortable seating designed for gathering and conversation.

### Kitchen
A beautifully appointed kitchen with premium finishes, generous prep space, and thoughtful organization for serious cooking and entertaining.

### Dining Room
An elegant space for family meals and dinner parties, designed to accommodate large gatherings while maintaining intimacy.

### Pantry
A well-organized pantry with ample storage for a fully stocked kitchen.

### Pool
A stunning pool area that serves as the centerpiece of outdoor living, perfect for relaxation and entertainment.

### Entertainment Room
A dedicated space for casual gathering, games, and relaxation with friends and family.

### Sports Bar
A fun and functional bar area perfect for game days and entertaining.

### Theater Room and Golf Simulator
A state-of-the-art entertainment space featuring both a home theater setup and golf simulator for year-round enjoyment.

### Bedrooms and Bathrooms
Multiple bedroom suites throughout the home, each designed with comfort and style in mind. Spa-inspired bathrooms feature premium fixtures and finishes.

## Design Inspiration

The "Rockaway Retreat" name evokes the laid-back coastal vibes of Rockaway Beach, translated into a desert setting. Key inspirations include:

- **Coastal Relaxation** - Easy, comfortable spaces that invite you to unwind
- **Natural Materials** - Woods and textures that connect to the outdoors
- **Light and Air** - Open layouts that maximize natural light
- **Entertainment** - Spaces designed for hosting and making memories
- **Indoor-Outdoor Living** - Taking advantage of Southern Utah's climate
